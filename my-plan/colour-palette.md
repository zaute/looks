# Your True Winter Colour Palette
**Season:** True Winter (confirmed by analysis)
**Why this season:** Cool pink undertone, fair skin, dark remaining hair/stubble, grey-blue eyes — you need high contrast, cool, and saturated colours. Anything warm or muted washes you out.

---

## Your Best Colours (wear near your face)
These will make your skin look balanced and your eyes stand out.

| Colour | Hex | Where to wear |
|--------|-----|---------------|
| Midnight Navy | `#191970` | Shirts, polos, jackets — your #1 colour |
| Charcoal | `#36454F` | T-shirts, knitwear, trousers |
| Deep Spruce Green | `#006400` | Shirts, polos — unexpected but very sharp on you |
| Burgundy / Oxford Blood | `#800000` | Shirts, knitwear, accents |
| Pure White | `#FFFFFF` | Shirts, t-shirts — high contrast with your colouring |
| True Black | `#000000` | Trousers, jackets, shoes |
| Icy Blue | `#A5C8D0` | Occasional shirt — complements grey-blue eyes |

---

## Accent Colours (accessories, scarves, pocket squares)
| Colour | Hex | How to use |
|--------|-----|------------|
| Silver / Steel | `#C0C0C0` | Watch, belt buckle, cufflinks — never gold |
| Deep Royal Blue | `#003153` | Pocket square, scarf |
| Crisp Red | `#CC0000` | Accent only — tie, pocket square |

---

## Your Wardrobe Neutrals (capsule foundation — buy these first)
Build everything around these three. Any combination works together.

1. **Charcoal** `#36454F` — trousers, knitwear, outerwear
2. **Midnight Navy** `#191970` — shirts, jackets, polos
3. **True Black** `#000000` — shoes, belts, trousers, outerwear

---

## Colours to Avoid
These clash with your cool undertone and drain your complexion.

| Colour | Why it doesn't work |
|--------|---------------------|
| Brown / Tan / Camel | Warm tones clash with your cool undertone — makes skin look grey |
| Mustard / Yellow / Orange | Too warm — washes out fair, cool skin completely |
| Warm Green (olive, khaki) | The yellow base fights your undertone |
| Gold jewellery / hardware | Cool silver always; gold reads wrong on your colouring |
| Off-white / Cream | Adds yellow tone near your face — use pure white instead |

> ⚠️ **The brown/yellow striped shirt flagged in your analysis falls into this category — retire it.**

## Your Starter Wardrobe Shopping List
Build this in order. Start with the basics.

### Phase 1 — Foundation (buy first)
- [ ] 2× Navy polo shirts (quality cotton or merino — this was specifically recommended in your plan)
- [ ] 1× Charcoal crew-neck knitwear (merino wool if budget allows)
- [ ] 1× Black chinos or trousers (well-fitted — not baggy)
- [ ] 1× Clean white t-shirt (well-fitted)

### Phase 2 — Expand
- [ ] 1× Navy unstructured blazer
- [ ] 1× Burgundy or deep green polo/shirt
- [ ] Dark denim jeans (slim or straight — nothing baggy)

### Phase 3 — Elevate
- [ ] A quality leather shoe or clean white sneaker (black or navy)
- [ ] Silver-toned watch
- [ ] Navy or charcoal outerwear (wool coat or quality jacket)

---

## The Simple Rule
> **Cool. Saturated. High contrast.**
> When in doubt: navy top + black or charcoal bottom = always correct for your colouring.
