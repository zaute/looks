# Your Fitness Plan
**Goal:** Improve posture, build upper body presence, make clothes fit better
**Programme:** 3 days/week — fits around any schedule

---

## What the Analysis Found
- **Rounded shoulders** (common from desk work at 55) — makes shirts pull at the back, reduces perceived height
- **Lean build with good symmetry** — you don't need to lose weight, you need to build upper back and shoulder width so clothes drape correctly
- **Forward head posture** — correcting this alone adds perceived height and authority

---

## The 3-Day Workout Plan

### Day 1 — Upper Body Push + Posture (Monday or similar)
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Face Pulls | 3×15 | **Do this first every session** — most important posture fix |
| Incline Dumbbell Press | 3×10 | Builds upper chest — fills shirt collar area |
| Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press | 3×10 | Broadens shoulder line |
| Cable Chest Fly | 3×12 | Shape, not bulk |
| Wall Angels | 2×10 | Feel the shoulder blades squeeze together |

### Day 2 — Lower Body + Core (Wednesday or similar)
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Goblet Squat | 3×12 | Knee-safe, builds legs so trousers fit |
| Romanian Deadlift (dumbbells) | 3×10 | Hamstrings + glutes — makes trousers sit correctly |
| Leg Press | 3×12 | No lower back stress |
| Plank | 3×30–45 sec | Core stability |
| Dead Bug | 3×8 each side | Lower back safe |

### Day 3 — Upper Body Pull + Posture (Friday or similar)
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Seated Cable Row | 3×12 | **Key exercise for your posture fix** — pulls shoulders back |
| Lat Pulldown | 3×10 | Creates the V-taper that makes waist look narrower |
| Dumbbell Rear Delt Fly | 3×15 | Eliminates the rounded-shoulder silhouette |
| Dumbbell Bicep Curl | 2×12 | Fills shirtsleeves |
| Band Pull-Apart | 3×20 | Cheap resistance band — do this at home too |

---

## Daily Posture Fix (10 min — do this every day, not just gym days)
1. **Chin Tucks** — 3×10 (counteracts forward head: chin in, not down)
2. **Doorway Chest Stretch** — 2×30 sec each side (opens tight chest)
3. **Band Pull-Aparts** — 3×20 (strengthens rear delts)
4. **Thoracic Extension over foam roller** — 2×60 sec (undoes desk-work rounding)

**Quick posture reminder for standing/walking:**
> "Shoulder blades squeezed together and down, pelvis slightly tucked"
Do this consciously whenever you're standing in a queue, on the phone, or at a counter.

## Why This Matters for Your Clothes
| Exercise | Clothing payoff |
|----------|----------------|
| Face pulls + Rows | Shirt back lies flat, no pulling across shoulders |
| Shoulder press | Jacket and shirt shoulders hang correctly |
| Lat pulldown | Creates V-shape — waist looks narrower |
| Core work | Trousers sit at natural waist, no belly protrusion |
| Posture correction | **Everything fits better** — adds perceived height, projects authority |

## The Lifestyle Foundation (Non-Negotiables)
These matter as much as the gym for how your body looks:

| Factor | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Protein** | 1.6–2.0g per kg bodyweight/day | Muscle cannot be retained without it. Eggs, meat, fish, dairy. |
| **Sleep** | 7–8 hours | Growth hormone releases during deep sleep. Under-sleeping destroys gym progress. |
| **Water** | 2–2.5 litres/day | Dehydration reduces performance, slows recovery, makes skin look worse. |
| **Ultra-processed food** | Reduce significantly | Drives belly fat and inflammation — visible faster than any exercise change. |

## Timeline: Honest Expectations
- **Week 1–2:** Posture starts improving (you'll feel it before you see it)
- **Week 4–6:** Shirts start fitting differently across the upper back
- **Week 8–12:** Visible shoulder and upper back development
- **Month 3+:** Clothes start fitting the way they're supposed to
