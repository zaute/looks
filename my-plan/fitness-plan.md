# Your Fitness Plan
**Goal:** Improve posture, build upper body presence, make clothes fit better
**Programme:** 3 days/week — fits around any schedule

---

## What the Analysis Found
- **Rounded shoulders** (common from desk work at 55) — makes shirts pull at the back, reduces perceived height
- **Lean build with good symmetry** — you don't need to lose weight, you need to build upper back and shoulder width so clothes drape correctly
- **Forward head posture** — correcting this alone adds perceived height and authority

---

## The 3-Day Workout Plan

> ⚠️ **Ved 55: konsistens slår intensitet.** Bedre å trene lett og regelmessig enn hardt og sporadisk. Aldri hopp over oppvarming.

### Oppvarming — 8 min (gjør dette FØR hver økt)
| Øvelse | Tid / Reps | Hvorfor |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Rolig gange eller sykling | 3 min | Øker blodgjennomstrømning til ledd |
| Arm circles (fremover + bakover) | 10 each | Varmer opp skulderleddet |
| Scapular retractions | 2×10 | Aktiverer øvre rygg før løft |
| Hip circles | 10 each side | Frigjør hofteleddet |
| Bodyweight squat | 2×10 | Aktiverer kne og ankel |

## Progressiv overbelastning — slik blir du sterkere
Uten progresjon stopper fremgangen. Følg denne regelen:

> **"2-for-2-regelen":** Hvis du klarer 2 ekstra reps på siste sett i 2 treninger på rad — øk vekten med 2–2.5 kg.

| Fase | Uke | Fokus |
|------|-----|-------|
| Tilvenning | 1–3 | Lær teknikken. Lett vekt. Ikke push. |
| Grunnbygging | 4–8 | Øk vekt når 2-for-2-regelen slår inn |
| Konsolidering | 9–12 | Hold vektene, øk kontroll og tempo |
| Deload | Hver 4.–5. uke | 1 uke med 50% vekt — kroppen bygger i hvile |

> ⚠️ **Deload er ikke lat — det er nødvendig.** Spesielt ved 55 trenger kroppen en uke lettere trening for å absorbere fremgangen.

## Nedtrapping + tøying — 8 min (etter hver økt)
Spesielt viktig for bryst og hofteleddsbøyer som strammer seg raskt.

| Tøying | Tid | Målmuskel |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Doorway chest stretch | 2×30 sek | Bryst + fremre skulder |
| Cross-body shoulder stretch | 2×30 sek each | Bakre skulder |
| Hip flexor stretch (knælende) | 2×30 sek each | Hofteleddsbøyer |
| Seated hamstring stretch | 2×30 sek each | Hamstrings |
| Thoracic extension over foam roller | 60 sek | Øvre rygg — motvirker rundryggen |
| Neck side stretch | 2×20 sek each | Nakke og øvre trapezius |

> 💡 **Tips:** Gjør thoracic extension hver dag — ikke bare treningsdager. Dette alene forbedrer holdningen raskere enn noe annet.

---

## Daily Posture Fix (10 min — do this every day, not just gym days)
1. **Chin Tucks** — 3×10 (counteracts forward head: chin in, not down)
2. **Doorway Chest Stretch** — 2×30 sec each side (opens tight chest)
3. **Band Pull-Aparts** — 3×20 (strengthens rear delts)
4. **Thoracic Extension over foam roller** — 2×60 sec (undoes desk-work rounding)

**Quick posture reminder for standing/walking:**
> "Shoulder blades squeezed together and down, pelvis slightly tucked"
Do this consciously whenever you're standing in a queue, on the phone, or at a counter.

## The Lifestyle Foundation (Non-Negotiables)
These matter as much as the gym for how your body looks:

| Factor | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Protein** | 1.6–2.0g per kg bodyweight/day | Muscle cannot be retained without it. Eggs, meat, fish, dairy. |
| **Sleep** | 7–8 hours | Growth hormone releases during deep sleep. Under-sleeping destroys gym progress. |
| **Water** | 2–2.5 litres/day | Dehydration reduces performance, slows recovery, makes skin look worse. |
| **Ultra-processed food** | Reduce significantly | Drives belly fat and inflammation — visible faster than any exercise change. |

---

## Foods & Snacks to Completely Avoid

These directly work against your goals — belly fat, skin redness, inflammation, poor sleep, and blocked muscle recovery.

### 🚫 The No-Go List

| Category | Examples | Why it's bad for you |
|----------|----------|----------------------|
| **Sugary drinks** | Coke, Pepsi, energy drinks, fruit juice, sweetened coffee | Spikes insulin → inflammation → worse skin redness. No nutrition, pure fat gain. |
| **Alcohol (especially beer)** | Beer, alcopops, cocktails | Belly fat specifically. Destroys sleep quality. Dehydrates skin. Blocks protein synthesis for 24–48 hrs. |
| **Crisps / chips** | Pringles, Doritos, all bagged crisps | Trans fats + salt + empty calories. Causes water retention → puffy face. |
| **Pastries & baked goods** | Croissants, muffins, donuts, cakes | Refined flour + sugar + seed oils. Drives inflammation and belly fat rapidly. |
| **Fast food** | McDonald's, kebab, pizza chain, fried chicken | Ultra-processed, trans fats, massive sodium. Single meal can undo days of effort. |
| **White bread / white pasta** | Sandwich bread, regular pasta in large amounts | High glycaemic — spikes blood sugar, drives fat storage. Swap for sourdough or whole grain. |
| **Sweets & chocolate bars** | Haribo, Snickers, KitKat, pick'n'mix | Pure sugar hit. Worsens skin redness, drives belly fat, crashes energy. |
| **Processed meats** | Salami, hot dogs, cheap sausages, deli meats | High sodium + preservatives → inflammation and water retention. |
| **Margarine & seed oils** | Margarine, sunflower oil in excess | High in omega-6 → promotes inflammation. Use olive oil instead. |
| **Breakfast cereals** | Most boxed cereals, granola bars | Hidden sugar — marketed as healthy, behaves like sweets. |

---

### ✅ What to Replace Them With
| Instead of | Have this |
|------------|-----------|
| Coke / juice | Sparkling water, black coffee, green tea |
| Crisps | Handful of nuts (almonds, walnuts), boiled eggs |
| Chocolate bar | Greek yoghurt + berries, dark chocolate 85%+ (small amount) |
| White bread | Sourdough, rye bread, or just skip it |
| Beer | Sparkling water with lime — or nothing |
| Fast food | Pre-cooked chicken + rice + veg (takes 5 min to assemble) |

> 💡 **The single biggest impact:** Cut sugary drinks and beer first. Everything else is secondary. These two changes alone visibly affect belly fat and skin within 3–4 weeks.
